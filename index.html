Goal-Based SIP Calculator – Plan Your Investments with Precision


 Planning for a specific financial goal—such as a child’s education, a dream vacation, or buying a home—requires knowing exactly how much to invest each month. Our Goal-Based SIP Calculator helps you quickly estimate the monthly Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) amount needed to reach your target corpus within a chosen time frame and expected return rate.

Simply enter three details:


  • Target Amount – the total savings you want to achieve.
  • Investment Period – the number of years you plan to invest.
  • Expected Annual Return – the average yearly return you anticipate from your mutual fund investments.


The calculator instantly tells you the required monthly SIP to meet your goal. This tool uses the standard SIP future value formula to project potential growth, helping you stay on track with your savings strategy.


Whether you’re planning a retirement fund, a child’s wedding, or a major purchase, a goal-based SIP ensures disciplined investing. By investing a fixed amount every month, you benefit from rupee-cost averaging and the power of compounding—two key factors in long-term wealth creation.
